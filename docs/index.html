About Samplescope App

Samplescope is cross-platform desktop app with the main goal to simplify audio samples search over internet sources. It helps quickly find great royalty-free sound effects you lookin for.

  • ✅ Access to over 650k sounds
  • ✅ High-Quality MP3 files

Main Features

Simple UI

Focus on your music not on complecated UIs, registrations, etc

Free Distribution

Open access to resources is important

Open-Source Project

App is transparent and open for improvements
